A new approach
to plant growth.

Our project this year involves the creation of a toolkit for the analysis of redox state. As new research continues to suggest, reactive oxygen species may play a huge role in a plethora of processes, including plant growth.

In our effort to advance this newfound science, we chose to create a hydroponics application that boosts plant growth and health by regulating oxidative stress levels in a plant roots’ environment - revolutionizing hydroponics farming.

We’re a synthetic biology research team introducing a new way of growing hydroponically.
